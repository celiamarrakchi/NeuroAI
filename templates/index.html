Diagnostic Capabilities

Our AI system accurately detects and classifies the following brain pathologies

Glioma

Gliomas originate in glial cells and represent the most common type of primary brain tumor. They range from low-grade to high-grade lesions. Our AI system identifies characteristic imaging patterns including irregular borders, infiltrative growth patterns, and perilesional edema.

  • Heterogeneous signal intensity detection
  • Infiltration boundary analysis
  • Edema quantification

Meningioma

Meningiomas arise from the dura mater and meningeal layers surrounding the brain and spinal cord. Typically benign and well-demarcated, these extra-axial tumors present characteristic imaging features. Our detection system recognizes dural attachment, well-defined margins, and enhancement patterns.

  • Dural tail identification
  • Boundary definition assessment
  • Enhancement characterization

Pituitary Adenoma

Pituitary adenomas develop within the pituitary gland at the sella turcica. These tumors can affect hormonal function and compress adjacent structures including the optic chiasm. Our AI analyzes sellar masses and evaluates relationships with surrounding anatomy.

  • Sellar mass localization
  • Suprasellar extension assessment
  • Compression evaluation

Normal Imaging

Normal brain imaging demonstrates intact neuroanatomy with no pathological findings. Our system establishes baseline normal patterns and confirms the absence of significant abnormalities, providing diagnostic confidence in negative studies.

  • Anatomical integrity verification
  • Normal pattern establishment
  • Negative finding confirmation

About This Project

NeuroRadiology AI represents a comprehensive approach to brain tumor detection using state-of-the-art deep learning models. Our system processes MRI and CT scans to identify, classify, and localize brain tumors with exceptional precision.

This project is the result of collaborative research combining expertise in artificial intelligence, medical imaging, and clinical radiology. We aim to democratize access to advanced diagnostic tools while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy and reliability.

By leveraging neural networks trained on extensive medical datasets, our platform assists healthcare providers in making informed decisions faster, ultimately improving patient care and outcomes across diverse clinical settings.
